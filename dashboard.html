About This Dashboard

Philly Stat 360 · Residential Vacancy Risk Model

What this shows

An interactive map of predicted vacancy risk scores for residential parcels across Philadelphia. The ward choropleth shows average risk by ward; zoom to level 13+ for individual parcel risk colors.

Model performance
AUC 0.940
Sensitivity 84.0%
Specificity 89.8%
Parcels scored ~436K

Calibrated 50/50 Logit + Random Forest ensemble. Reported on a stratified 30% test holdout at the Youden-optimal threshold. Block-CV (5-fold by census tract) AUC is 0.968; ZIP-grouped CV AUC is 0.888.

Data sources

  • L&I Violations & Clean & Seal — OpenDataPhilly
